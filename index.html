The Problem: Information Overload & Muddled Thinking

In today's world, we're bombarded with "information" distracting us the problems we are trying to solve, misinformation, disinformation, malinformation. This often leads to fragmented understanding, difficulty in evaluating arguments, and ultimately, poor decision-making. We struggle to see the bigger picture amidst the noise, making collaborative problem-solving and productive discourse challenging. While all the large AI companies are chasing the Sword (God as a Machine), Claritrie is taking a lateral direction towards the Shield (Global Philosopher & Responsible Secretary)

The Solution: Clarity Through Structure & Transparency

Claritrie provides "Maps" for our shared thoughts. We're starting the journey with Argument Maps, and making our way through Problem maps to Solution Maps! We can't make intricate roads without creating pathways first! Just as GPS helps you navigate physical terrain, Claritrie helps you chart complex information landscapes. We offer tools to visually map arguments, identify key points, to help better decision-making. This structured approach transforms information chaos into coherent insight.

How It Works

Map Arguments Visually

Deconstruct complex topics into clear, interconnected visual maps. Identify premises, counter-arguments, & evidence with ease. This is about breaking down problems into the smallest fragment, the essence being the claim. Interpretable, Focused, Concise.

AI Curator

Claritrie has no external dependencies/APIs, it's systems can also be ran offline using in-house Generative AI to filter out the Noise, this is not a bot-playground! Leverage intelligent algorith-NO Data-Mining! This AI's goal is to be as transparent as possible, telling you what and why, to help organize information, find causal & correlated issues, and identify logical fallacies, rapidly advancing collective analysis of humanity's current challenges.

Human-Centric Verification

While AI assists, human intellect remains central. Tools are designed to augment, not replace, critical thinking and collaborative validation. Everything starts by being humane and ends for humanity, profit/ad-revenue does not.

Vision: Protecting & Improving Human Discourse

In an age of automated and unwavering misinformation/polarization tools, fostering clear, reasoned, and empathetic communication is paramount. Our tools are built to empower individuals and groups to engage thoughtfully with complex issues, promoting understanding and constructive dialogue for a healthier society. Verify & Evaluate evidence, and illuminate pathways to clearer understanding and sounder conclusions. Also, since we're concerned about human problems, why not make Bot & Comment systems less relevant along the way?!
